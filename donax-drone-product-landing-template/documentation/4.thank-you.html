Thank You!

Thanks for using Donax. We take great pride in creating solid templates and strongly believe in robust customer service practices. Should you have a question that neither this documentation file nor Themeforest’s forums can resolve and we’ll do our very best to assist you.

If you like our template, please take 1 minute to rate it on Themeforest:

  1. Go to Themeforest and login with your credentials.
  2. Hover on your account name in the top right corner of the screen. Click Downloads link and you will see your purchases list.
  3. Click stars next to our template to leave your rating.

It will help us a lot to keep being motivated to release new template updates and improvements for free.

We hope you will enjoy using it for your next project!
